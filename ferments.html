Lacto-Fermented Red Plums

Captured at the peak of late summer, these plums undergo a transformative brine process that yields a deep umami profile with a vibrant, acidic finish.

Laboratory Parameters

Salinity 2.5%
Temp 20°C
Time 7 Days

The Brine Mechanics

Lacto-fermentation relies on the suppression of putrefactive bacteria while fostering Lactobacillus growth. The precise salinity of 2.5% ensures cellular structural integrity while promoting a clean, acidic profile.

Salinity Concentration 2.5%
0.0% Optimal Range 10.0%
science

Scientific Note

Red plums contain high levels of anthocyanins. During the 7-day cycle at 20°C, the pigment leaches into the brine, creating a neon-pink syrup that is as versatile as the fruit itself.

Components

  • Organic Red Plums 1000g // Firm & Ripe
  • Unrefined Sea Salt 25g // Non-Iodized
  • Filtered Water q.s. // Chlorine-free

The Methodology

01
Preparation & Cleansing

Wash the fruit thoroughly in cold water. Halve the plums and remove the pits. Maintaining skin integrity is vital for the final texture.

02
The Osmotic Draw

Toss plums with the weighted salt. Let sit for 30 minutes to begin the osmotic release of natural juices before submerging in the vessel.

03
The 7-Day Cycle

Seal the vessel with a fermentation weight to ensure full submersion. Store in a dark space at a constant 20°C. Monitor pH levels daily until a target of 3.4 is reached.
